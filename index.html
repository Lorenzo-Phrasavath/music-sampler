The Musical Film World of
Danny Elfman

Danny Elfman is an American film composer who has written the music behind some of the world’s most popular movies. Famous for his collaborations with film director Tim Burton, Danny Elfman’s music has been integral to helping Burton’s weird and wacky characters jump off the screen. Elfman’s compositions have a distinct feeling of fantasy and are often characterized as being spooky or mysterious. Without Danny Elfman, we wouldn’t have the distinct music behind such movies as: Pee-wee’s Big Adventure (1985), Beetlejuice (1988), Batman (1989), Edward Scissorhands (1990), The Nightmare Before Christmas (1993), Goodwill Hunting (1997), Men in Black (1997), Spider-Man (2002), or even The Avengers (2015).
